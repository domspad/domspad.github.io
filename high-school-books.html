Great Books Are Hard

Sun 14 June 2015 By Dominic Spadacene

Looks like I wasn't the only person to have trouble finishing any "great" book in high school.

A book that is both 'required' reading and in the top 199 best books ever (as voted on by Goodreads readers) has a ~60% chance of also being in the top 199 of the 'Started but Unfinished' list...

The distribution of ratings given to books from these lists are shown below. Interestingly the ratings from the unfinished list and the required-readings list are quite similar.
