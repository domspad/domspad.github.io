Simple Rules for Browsing

Mon 10 August 2015 By Dominic Spadacene

In light of watching a video on Simple Rules - Simple solutions to Complex Systems, I've decided to try out mapping out some simple rules governing behaviour in various tasks I engage in. Below I try out the task of researching a topic on the internet.

  1. No aimless distractions (sorry Reddit, Facebook, Twitter, Gmail)
  2. Less than 5 windows open, each with less than 10 tabs
  3. Each window has its own theme
  4. Fill out one window at a time (no jumping between themes)
